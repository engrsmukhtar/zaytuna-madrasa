Islamiyyah Section.

Learn the Glorious Qur'an, it's Exegesis and develop a solid understanding of the deen.

These are held from 8:30am to 1:30pm on both Saturdays and Sundays and by 4:00pm to 6:00pm on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays.

Hifzh/Weekend Section.

Memorise the Glorious Qur'an while studying the deen.

The Weekend Section entails memorising the Glorious Qur'an, learning Tajweed and studying some Islamic Subjects. It runs from 8:30am to 2:20pm on both Saturdays and Sundays.

Women Islamiyyah.

Special classes for the women in our society to learn and study conveniently.

This section is especially designed for the women in our society to learn about the Glorious Qur'an and develop the knowledge of the deen. The classes are on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays from 11:00am to 1:00pm
